By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

This App does not collect, store, or process any personal data on our own servers. However, we use third-party services, Google AdMob and Google Play Games Services, which may collect data for purposes such as advertising and gameplay functionality. These services may process your information as described below:

a. AdMob (Google Ad Services)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect information to provide personalized and non-personalized ads, depending on your location and consent preferences. Types of data AdMob may collect include:

  • Device Information: Device identifiers, IP addresses, and operating system details.
  • Location Information: AdMob may collect location data to deliver targeted ads, if consent is provided.

For more information, please review AdMob’s Privacy Policy.

b. Google Play Games Services

The App integrates with Google Play Games Services, which provides features like achievements and leaderboards. Google Play Games may collect gameplay data, including:

  • Player Profile Information: Username, profile picture, and other account details from your Google account.
  • Game Performance Data: Achievements, scores, and leaderboard standings.

For further details, please review Google Play Games’ Privacy Policy.

2. User Consent Management

We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to gather your consent preferences before displaying ads. UMP will present you with options to consent to personalized or non-personalized ads, and we will initialize AdMob based on the choices you make.

Personalized Ads: If you choose to receive personalized ads, AdMob may use your data to deliver ads tailored to your interests.

Non-Personalized Ads: If you opt for non-personalized ads, AdMob will serve generic ads that are not based on your personal data.

3. How Information is Used

The third-party services in this App may use your data as follows:

  • Advertising (AdMob): To deliver and personalize ads based on your consent preferences.
  • Gameplay Features (Google Play Games Services): To enable features such as leaderboards, achievements, and player statistics.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

As the developer of this App, we do not collect, store, or share any user data. All data processing is managed by Google through AdMob and Google Play Games Services. Any information collected is handled according to Google’s privacy practices.

5. User Rights and Controls

  • Google Play Games Profile: Control the visibility of your Google Play Games profile within the Google Play Games app.
